# Interactive Floor Planner

**Live Site:** https://edperez0.github.io/floor-planner/

Plan furniture layouts on your floor plan with accurate scaling and real-world dimensions.

---

## How to Use

### 1. Upload Your Floor Plan

**Drag & Drop:**
- Drag any floor plan image (PNG, JPG, etc.) onto the page

**Or use the file picker:**
- Click **"📁 Upload Floor Plan"** in the top toolbar
- Select your image

💾 Your floor plan is automatically saved to your browser and will reload on your next visit.

---

### 2. Calibrate the Scale

This step ensures furniture dimensions match real-world sizes.

1. Click **"Calibrate Scale"** in the toolbar
2. **First click** on your floor plan to start drawing a line
3. **Second click** to finish the line (draw along a wall or dimension you know)
4. Enter the **real-world length** of that line:
   - Example: 10 foot wall = **10 feet, 0 inches**
   - Example: 8 foot 6 inch wall = **8 feet, 6 inches**
5. Click **"Set Scale"**

**Tip:** Use the longest dimension you can find on your floor plan for best accuracy.

---

### 3. Add Furniture

**Quick Add with Presets:**
- Queen Bed (5' × 6'8")
- King Bed (6'4" × 6'8")
- Sofa (7' × 3')
- Dining Tables (6-seat, 8-seat)
- Coffee Table, TV Stand, Desk
- Rugs (5×7, 8×10)

Just click any preset button and it appears on your floor plan.

**Custom Furniture:**
1. Click **"+ Add Custom"**
2. Enter name (e.g., "Couch", "Bookshelf")
3. Enter width and depth in feet and inches
4. Click **"Add to Canvas"**

---

### 4. Arrange Your Furniture

**Move:**
- Click and drag any furniture piece

**Rotate:**
- Select furniture (click on it)
- Drag the circular rotation handle at the top

**Resize:**
- Select furniture
- Drag any corner handle to resize

**Edit Exact Dimensions:**
- Select furniture
- Change dimensions in the right panel
- Click **"Update Dimensions"**

**Delete:**
- Select furniture
- Click **"Delete"** in the right panel

---

### 5. Change Floor Plans

To work on a different floor plan:
- Click **"📁 Change Floor Plan"** in the toolbar
- Upload new image
- **Important:** Recalibrate for the new floor plan

---

## Tips

✅ **Calibrate on long walls** for better accuracy  
✅ **Use preset furniture** for standard sizes  
✅ **Your floor plan auto-saves** to your browser  
⚠️ **Floor plans are device-specific** - upload again on other devices  
⚠️ **Don't clear browser data** or your floor plan will be lost

---

## Troubleshooting

**Can't calibrate?**  
→ Make sure you uploaded a floor plan first

**Furniture too big/small?**  
→ Recalibrate using a longer line with accurate dimensions

**Floor plan disappeared?**  
→ Check if you cleared browser cache or switched devices - re-upload if needed

**Can't drag furniture?**  
→ Make sure you completed calibration first
